Internet Communication

What is Internet communication

Internet communication refers to communicating with people over the internet. It could be in any form: messages, voice, or video calls. One of the major advantages of internet communication over traditional communication is cost savings. In addition, there are a lot of free tools that make internet communication easier, such as WhatsApp, Skype, Google Meet, and Messenger. Even businesses can communicate with overseas customers over the phone at pocket-friendly prices using the internet.
